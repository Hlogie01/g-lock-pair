CottonFest class of 2024

Celebrate South African Music Culture With Us at cottonFest

Experience A World Class Music Event At cottonFest

Get ready for an unforgettable night as CottonFest_Org together with G-lock Events proudly presents the spectacular CottonFest, live at Newtown Junction, JHB, South Africa, on the 31st of August 2024! This vibrant event celebrates all genres of music, paying tribute to the life and legacy of the late music icon and founder, Riky Rick. Join us for a night of electrifying performances, diverse musical acts, and a celebration of unity through music.

Don’t miss your chance to be part of this monumental event—grab your tickets now and join us for a night of unforgettable music and celebration that honors the diverse sounds of South Africa and the legacy of Riky Rick!
