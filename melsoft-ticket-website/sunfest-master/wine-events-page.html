Indaba Hotel Tasting Room

The event will take place at the prestigious Indaba Hotel, situated in the heart of Johannesburg. Renowned for its blend of luxury and comfort, Indaba Hotel offers a serene and sophisticated setting, making it the perfect venue for our exclusive wine tasting event.

The room is equipped with everything needed to host an exceptional wine tasting event, ensuring a memorable experience for all attendees.

Located amidst lush gardens and offering top-notch amenities, the Indaba Hotel is not only a premier destination for events but also a retreat for those seeking relaxation and indulgence. The hotel’s dedication to impeccable service and attention to detail ensures that every guest enjoys a truly exceptional visit.

Hosted by Cavalli Wine Estate Stellenbosch

As a 'destination brand' renowned for its wine, cuisine, design, architecture, indigenous landscaping, and art, Cavalli has developed a loyal following of discerning patrons. The estate encompasses 100 hectares of prime property located in the prestigious Helderberg region of Stellenbosch, South Africa.

Given our esteemed location in Stellenbosch, we are pleased to announce our partnership with G-LOC Events Group and Indaba Hotel to bring our award-winning wines to Johannesburg, ensuring they are accessible for all to enjoy.

About the Organizers

Cavalli Wine Estate: Dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in every aspect of its operations, Cavalli Wine Estate offers award-winning wines, exceptional culinary experiences, and stunning design. Nestled in the Helderberg region, Cavalli prides itself on sustainable practices and a commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the landscape.

G-LOC Events Group: Renowned for its expertise in event management and production, G-LOC Events Group brings a wealth of experience to the partnership. Known for organizing high-profile events and creating memorable experiences, G-LOC is committed to delivering exceptional service and ensuring the success of every event.

Indaba Hotel: As the prestigious venue for this event, Indaba Hotel offers a perfect blend of luxury and convenience. Located in Johannesburg, Indaba Hotel is known for its world-class facilities, impeccable service, and warm hospitality. This partnership ensures a seamless and extraordinary experience for all attendees.

Together, Cavalli Wine Estate, G-LOC Events Group, and Indaba Hotel aim to provide a unique and unforgettable experience, combining the best of Stellenbosch's wine heritage with Johannesburg's vibrant energy. This collaboration is a celebration of excellence, quality, and the joy of fine wine.
